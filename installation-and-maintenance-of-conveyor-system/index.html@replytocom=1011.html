Installation and Maintenance of Conveyor System

9 Comments / Blog

A conveyor system is one of the most important pieces of machine usually found in the beverage and pharmaceutical industries. They are machines designed to move bottles and pallets from one place to another. They are widely used in beverage industries such as beer, wine, juice, etc. The main purpose of these machines is to increase productivity and reduce labour costs.


In order to ensure the smooth operation of a production line, it is necessary to install a conveyor. The installation of a conveyor system is a complex task. If you don’t have enough experience, you may face some problems during the installation process. A conveyor system consists of three major components: a conveyor belt, a motor, and a frame. In addition, there are other parts such as a guide rail, a support beam, a safety guard, and a control panel.


All conveyor systems must be installed in accordance with national and local safety compliance. In the event that any caution or warning labels affixed to the equipment are damaged in transit of the equipment to the site, you should order the appropriate replacement labels before installing the equipment. As the system is assembled, check all fasteners to ensure that they are secure, even those that are not connected with the assembly of the sections.


Conveyor systems should be maintained as much as they are being used. Cleaning them regularly helps prevent bacteria growth and ensures they operate smoothly. Cleaning the conveyor once every two weeks is enough. The cleaning procedure involves removing debris and dust particles from the conveyor belt. This can be done manually or automatically. Correct lubrication is essential to obtain the long life of the chain drive. Also, it is always good to keep spare components at your facility so that they don’t have to wait for long intervals of time when something breaks down.


In conclusion, it’s important to know what type of bottle conveyor you need and to understand the maintenance requirements.
